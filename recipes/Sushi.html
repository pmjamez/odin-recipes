How to make Sushi!

Dragon Roll Description:

The Dragon Roll is one of the most popular "american" sushi rolls. Each sushi restuarant has their own spin on it, but it is usually a combination of unagi (broiled eel), avocado, and tempura shrimp. More popular amongst those who cannot eat raw fish, the dragon roll is symbolic due to its tasty ingredients.

Ingredients:

    Sushi Rice
    Seaweed
    Tempura Shrimp
    Broiled Eel
    Avocado
    Eel Sauce
    Sesame Seeds

Steps:

  • First, prepare all your ingradients by slicing avocaodo, heating up your eel, frying your shrimp, and cooking your rice
  • Now lay out all your ingredients so that they are visible and easy to grab
  • Next, grab a half sized nori sheet and spread an even layer of sushi rice
  • Flip it rice side down and begin filling with 2 tempura shrimp with the tails sticking out and 2 large avocado slices
  • Carefully roll the sushi using a bamboo mat, making sure the sushi roll is nice and sealed
  • Top your roll with a whole eel filet and press it down firmly with your bamboo mat
  • Now slice your rolls into 8 pieces, top with Eel sauce and sesame seeds and you're done!
